About Me

I am a Front-end Web Developer and Content Writer with a master’s degree from American University. I am conversant in multiple development languages and tools, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and jQuery. I acquired these skills after attending a six month Coding Bootcamp offered by Rutgers University.

I am excellent at both written and verbal communication and I am trained in cultural sensitivity and conflict resolution. I’m resourceful, organized, and work effectively both in team environments and independently. I enjoy creating user friendly and stylish websites for clients that further important causes.

I am looking to apply my experience and expertise at front-end web development on behalf of a client whose mission I can believe in.
